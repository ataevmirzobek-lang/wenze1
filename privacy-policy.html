Privacy Policy for Wenze Investments LLC

Effective Date: [02/02/2026]

Wenze Investments LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose,
 and safeguard your information when you engage with our trucking, freight, and logistics services, or interact with our website or communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and billing information.

Business Information: Company name, business address, and shipping or logistics details.

Driver & Vehicle Information: Driver’s license details, vehicle registration, insurance documentation, safety records, and compliance-related information.

Technical & Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and website interaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Provide trucking, freight, and logistics services.

Communicate with customers, drivers, brokers, and partners.

Process payments and invoices.

Maintain regulatory and legal compliance.

Improve our operations, safety standards, and customer experience.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

With trusted service providers who assist with operations, payment processing, or compliance.

When required by law, regulation, subpoena, or government request.

In connection with a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration,
 or disclosure. While no system is completely secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

Request access to your personal information.

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.

Opt out of certain communications.

Requests can be made using the contact information below.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance functionality and analyze usage. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective when posted, with the updated effective date listed above.
